Prevent Water Pollution

Water Pollution is caused by substances or waste that people dump into large bodies of water. Some examples of pollutants include: industrial waste, sewage, radioactive waste, oil pollution, underground storage leakages, acid rain, and eutrophication. Water pollution is a big problem because it is harming the planet’s sea life and our own water sources. If the problem goes unsolved, our drinking water will be at risk. Here, we present more information about what water pollution is and how to prevent it, in order to save our planet.
