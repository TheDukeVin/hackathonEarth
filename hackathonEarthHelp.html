How Can I Help?

You can make a difference by:

  • Throw Trash Away
  • Conserve water by turning off the tap and showers so that there is less contaminated water that needs to be cleaned
  • Be careful with what what put in the sink or toilet
  • Don’t litter
  • Plant trees and flowers
  • Use less plastic, use more biodegradeable materials
  • Being Cautious of Garbage Disposal
  • Pick up Litter
  • Raise Awareness of this problem by contacting your local community service groups
  • Reuse, Reduce, Recycle
